Our Story

Bred with island pride & passion

Borinquen Kennels is a female-owned XL Bully operation based right here in Puerto Rico. We're not just breeders — we're a family dedicated to raising healthy, structurally correct, and temperament-tested American XL Bullies.

Every dog in our program is health tested, socialized from birth, and raised with love. We produce dogs that represent the best of the breed — big, blocky, beautiful, and gentle with family. Proudly serving Puerto Rico and the entire Caribbean.
